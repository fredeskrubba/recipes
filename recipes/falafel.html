Falafel

Description:

A recipe for falafels, the iconic middle eastern vegetarian dish.

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • parsley
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Potato
  • Eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • Chili
  • Lemon
  • Frying Oil

Steps

  1. Blend the ingredients
  2. Make round balls from the mashed ingredients
  3. Deepfry the balls
  4. Serve with lemon
