Lasagna

Description:

A recipe for lasagna, the iconic Italian dish everyone loves.

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Squash
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • red Wine
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry garlic and onion in the olive oil
  2. Brown the meat and add the rest of the ingredients
  3. Assemble the lasgna
  4. Top it with cheese
